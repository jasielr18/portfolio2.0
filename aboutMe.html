About Me

I’m a self-driven web developer with a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. After completing a year-long programming course at INFOTEP, I’ve been building real projects to sharpen my skills and transition into a professional role in tech.

I have professional experience in customer service and team leadership, having worked as a Technical Support Agent at Teleperformance and currently as a Supervisor at Alorica. These roles have strengthened my communication skills, adaptability, and problem-solving—qualities I now apply to web development.

I’m fluent in English and comfortable working in bilingual environments. Currently, I’m focused on creating responsive websites and interactive web applications, and I’m actively looking for opportunities to grow as a front-end developer.
